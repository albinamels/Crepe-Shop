Welcome to our restaurant

Blinis are traditional East European pancakes with a history that dates back over a millennium. Blini (the multiple of “blin”) in their innumerable variety are among the most beloved Eastern European dishes today.

Plain or topped with jam, honey, fruit, or filled with sweet and savory ingredients - blini have been the favorite comfort food for generations. We look forward to introducing the Palisadians and other Angelinos to this wonderful culinary tradition.

Blin Blin will offer unique and delicious breakfast and lunch alternative for local community.
